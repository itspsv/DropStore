1. marinate the chicken: chicken, masalas, kasuri methi, lemon, curd, ginger garlic paste, salt, kothimeera
2. oil in pan and fry chicken pieces 
3. take them out and smoke them with ghee and coconut shell.
4. make a paste of onions, ginger, garlic, green chillies, kothimeera
5. add oil and fry some onions and add the remaining marinated mixture and fry 
6. add cashew paste and required salt, chilli powder
7. add the smoked chicken and water and cook. 
8. add kasuri methi, coriander.
